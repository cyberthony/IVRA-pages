IVRA–VRA: Air quality maps aligned with the revised AAQD

The revised Ambient Air Quality Directive (AAQD, EU-2024/2881) introduces stricter limit and target values. As a result, the colour scales used in our plots need to be updated to keep exceedances clearly visible and consistent across years.

Our current palette was introduced in 2022 at the start of the review process and has been used for three consecutive years. Over this period, air quality in Europe has generally improved, which changes how concentrations are distributed and how exceedances should stand out on the maps.

For the 2025 dataset (the first year covered by the revised AAQD), we therefore revise the colour scales so that exceedances of the new limit and target values are systematically highlighted (yellow/brown), ensuring a robust and comparable visual interpretation.
